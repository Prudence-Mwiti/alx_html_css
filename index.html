Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a session?

You can book a session through our website or contact us directly via phone or email.

2. Are online sessions available?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online counseling options.

3. What issues do you specialize in?

We specialize in stress, anxiety, depression, relationship issues, trauma, and self-esteem challenges.

4. Is therapy confidential?

Absolutely. We uphold strict confidentiality in all our sessions as per ethical guidelines.
